Privacy and Data Protection Policy

Alliance Rep Brasil is committed to protecting the privacy and personal data of our customers and visitors. This Privacy and Data Protection Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your personal data in accordance with the General Data Protection Law (LGPD).

Collected Information

We collect personal information when you register on our website, subscribe to our newsletter, place an order, or fill out a form. The information we collect includes your name, email address, physical address, phone number, and other relevant information to process your order or enhance your experience on our site.

Use of Information

We use your personal information to process your orders, respond to your inquiries, improve our services, and send you our promotions and marketing information. We do not share your information with third parties except when necessary to process your orders or comply with legal obligations.

Data Security

We use technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, misuse, loss, or disclosure. We store your information on secure servers and limit access to personal information only to employees who need to access it.

Your Privacy Rights

You have the right to request access, correction, or deletion of your personal information at any time. You may also opt-out of receiving our marketing communications. Please contact us to exercise your privacy rights.

Changes to this Policy

We may update this Privacy and Data Protection Policy periodically to reflect changes in our data collection and use practices. We recommend that you check this page regularly to stay informed about our policies.
